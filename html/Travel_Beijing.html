Beijing/北京

My travel:

I went to Beijing serveral times. Last time was 2018 summer by high speed rail.

Places need to visit:

1.The Great Wall of China: This world-famous landmark is one of the most popular destinations in Beijing, featuring stunning views and historic significance.
2.Forbidden City: This is a UNESCO World Heritage site, once the imperial palace of the Ming and Qing dynasties, and now a major museum of Chinese history and culture.
3.Temple of Heaven: This is an ancient temple and UNESCO World Heritage site that served as a place of worship for emperors of the Ming and Qing dynasties.
4.Summer Palace: A UNESCO World Heritage site, this imperial garden is a beautiful park with stunning pavilions, bridges, and lakes.
5.Tiananmen Square: One of the largest public squares in the world, this is a historic site that witnessed many significant events in China's modern history.
6.Beihai Park: A beautiful park with a rich history, featuring gardens, temples, and a lake with boating and fishing activities.
7.Hutongs: These are traditional alleys and courtyard houses that represent the old Beijing lifestyle and culture.
8.798 Art District: A trendy neighborhood with galleries, studios, and shops, offering an exciting blend of contemporary art and culture.
