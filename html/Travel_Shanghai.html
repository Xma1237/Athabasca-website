Shanghai/上海

My travel:

I went to Shanghai in 2017 summer by high speed rail.I stayed in my friend's home located near Oriental Pearl Tower. I stayed in Shanghai for 4 days.

Places need to visit:

1.The Bund: A scenic promenade along the Huangpu River, featuring colonial-era architecture and stunning views of the city skyline.
2.Yu Garden: A classical Chinese garden with a history of more than 400 years, located in the heart of the Old City.
3.Shanghai Tower: The tallest building in China and the second-tallest in the world, offering panoramic views of the city from its observation deck.
4.Shanghai World Financial Center: A skyscraper with a unique trapezoidal aperture at the top, providing stunning views from its observation deck.
5.Nanjing Road: A popular shopping street in Shanghai, featuring high-end stores, restaurants, and entertainment.
6.Jing'an Temple: A Buddhist temple located in the heart of the city, known for its beautiful architecture and peaceful atmosphere.
7.Tianzifang: A maze of alleyways and shops in the French Concession, filled with art galleries, cafes, and boutique stores.
8.Oriental Pearl Tower: A famous landmark of Shanghai, featuring a panoramic view of the city from its observation deck and a revolving restaurant.
