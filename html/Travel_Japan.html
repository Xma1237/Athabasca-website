Japan/日本

My Plan:

I plan travel to Japan in 2024 summer. Hope I can stay there over two weeks.

Places I'm planning to visit:

1.Tokyo: The capital city of Japan, famous for its blend of traditional and modern culture, including landmarks such as the Tokyo Tower, the Imperial Palace, and the Meiji Shrine.
2.Kyoto: A city in central Japan with a rich history and traditional culture, including famous temples, shrines, and gardens such as the Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kiyomizu-dera Temple, and the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove.
3.Mount Fuji: Japan's highest mountain and one of its most iconic landmarks, known for its stunning natural beauty and hiking trails.
4.Hokkaido: Japan's northernmost island, known for its stunning natural beauty, including hot springs, national parks, and winter sports.
5.Okinawa: A tropical island chain in the southernmost part of Japan, known for its beautiful beaches, coral reefs, and unique culture.
